Adhyay 02

Pehla "I Love You" - Dil Ki Sachi Dhadkan

Maine pehle kaha. Kai baar try kiya tha, lekin dar tha - kahin main usey na kho doon. Lekin jab dil bhar gaya, toh keh diya. Usne response nahi diya turant.

"Woh khush thi, muskurai bhi... lekin mujhe wait karwaya jawab ke liye. Bohot lamba wait kiya maine, lekin jab usne bhi 'Love You' kaha - us din laga sab kuch poora ho gaya."

Raat ko aankh nahi lagti thi. Jab woh puuchti thi "Soye?" - main jhooth bolta tha "Haan". Lekin sach yeh tha ki main uski photos dekhta rehta tha, uske messages, humari baatein, uske smile ke screenshots - subah tak.

Woh roye toh mujse control nahi hota tha. Andar se rota tha, usey hug karna chahta tha, uski aankhon ke aansu pochna chahta tha. Uske rone par mujhe gussa aata tha - kyunki main usey dukhi nahi dekh sakta.

Adhyay 04

Ladai, Maafi Aur Phir Pyaar

"Main jaanta hoon hum ek dusre ko kabhi nahi chhodenge. Kabhi nahi. Yeh mera bharosa hai, yeh mera promise hai."

Ladai ke baad main bohot emotional ho jaata hoon. Khud ko hurt karta hoon, terrace par jaata hoon, rota hoon, khana nahi khata, paani nahi peeta. Uski photos dekhta rehta hoon subah tak. Uska reply, call, message - sab ka intezaar.

Woh mujhe gussa hoti hai jab main properly baat nahi karta, jab apni health ka khayal nahi rakhta. Woh care karti hai, isliye gussa karti hai.

Adhyay 05

Darr Hai, Lekin Vishwas Zyada Hai

Sach kahun? Mujhe darr lagta hai woh mujhe chhod de. Yeh soch ke hi main skeleton jaisa feel karta hoon - body bina soul ke, heart bina feelings ke.

Long distance ka darr nahi hai - main usey trust karta hoon. Lekin mere weight ka darr hai. Main overweight hoon, try kar raha hoon reduce karne ka. Kabhi kabhi rota hoon apni body dekh ke.

"Mujhe darr hai - kya main fail ho jaunga? Kya main UK mein job nahi paunga? Kya main usse khush nahi rakh paunga? Main family bana chahta hoon, paisa kamaana chahta hoon, usey happy rakhna chahta hoon poori zindagi."

Family approval ka darr bhi hai kabhi kabhi. Uske dadaji - unse darr lagta hai. Kya woh manenge? Kya woh Tulsi ko emotional blackmail karenge?

Raat ko overthink karta hoon. Yeh mera sabse bada problem hai. Sochta rehta hoon - par sach yeh hai ki main us par bharosa rakhta hoon, woh mujh par rakhti hai.

Adhyay 06

Woh Yaadein Jo Dil Mein Hamesha Rahengi

Har pal romantic hai humari kahani mein. Jab uski mummy maan gayi mere baare mein, jab maine sapna dekha humari shaadi ka...

"Usne mujhe video call par ek kiss di thi - sirf ek baar. Main roz wait karta hoon doosri kiss ka. Woh kehti hai 'No' aur phir hasti hai. Yehi hai meri duniya."

Call par soti hai woh kabhi kabhi. Main usey dekhta rehta hoon soyi hui. Uska chehra kitna innocent lagta hai, kitna pyaara. Tab aur pyaar ho jaata hai.

Usne surprises bhi bheje hain - mobile phone, Apple earphones, wallet, fruits, trimmer, snacks, books, heart pillow, ice cream, roses, cakes... main count nahi kar sakta.

Normal din hota hai, delivery boy aata hai - main hasti hoon, usey yaad karti hoon. Thank you Tulsi. Lekin baccha, zyada paisa mat kharch karo - ek baar mahine mein theek hai.
