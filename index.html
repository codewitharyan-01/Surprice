08. Questions

FAQ

Wispa AI is designed for all-day usage with a single charge, offering 12-16 hours of active use depending on interaction frequency.

Currently, Wispa connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth and uses mobile data. Phase 01 of our roadmap includes direct internet connectivity for standalone operation.

Wispa AI integrates with ChatGPT and Google Gemini at launch, with plans to support additional AI platforms based on user demand.

All voice data is encrypted end-to-end. We do not store recordings on our servers—only the processed text is sent to AI providers following their privacy policies.

Bluetooth 5.0 provides up to 40 meters (130 feet) of range in open spaces, though walls and obstacles may reduce this distance.

Each Wispa device is paired to one phone at a time, but you can easily switch between devices through the mobile app.
